Craft CMS is one of the fastest content management system that enables us to develop and design any website from the simplest to the complicated one providing a very user-friendly interface for the users to manage the content.
Here are some reasons why we use this extensible, scalable and highly-configurable CMS as our of one of the go-to CMS platforms:

Beautiful and simple design

Creating and managing content is a daily basis task, and the platform you are using should not be complex to learn and use, and the design should be easier than you think. Craft CMS is very simple in design yet beautiful and elegant. It is concise and clear with a highly responsive design.

Live previews

This is one of the beautiful features of Craft CMS. As the users make edits, it gets displayed automatically for a preview in a separate live preview window. You do not have to save the content for the preview as it appears as you go. Therefore, you can share your draft with your team as a content manager or test your content draft on any device like a smartphone or tablet using Craft CMS generated preview URL.

Flexibility

Content editing is super flexible with Craft CMS for the end users, without compromising on the design and final appearance of the product. You can add images, videos, quotes, infographics or any other rich media along with your text.

Support

Craft CMS has a great online support and community support for developers and end users.

Easy user management

You can manage your users with Craft CMS’ easy and simple interface. You can also streamline your public user registration process, customize and create dashboards as you want, insert an RSS feed, include a quick post module to your website and more along with user management through Craft CMS.
